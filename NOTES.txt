Занят порт:
в powershell=>

taskkill /f /im node.exe

/f - force ended

/im - Specifies the image name of the process to be terminated.

node.exe - executable file

или попробовать
"scripts": {
    "stop-win": "Taskkill /IM node.exe /F",
    "stop-linux": "killall node"
},
